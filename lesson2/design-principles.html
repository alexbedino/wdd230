White Space and Clean Design

Shopify

The use of white space on the homepage of Shopify is really done masterfully as it is a tool which brings the attention of the user to actionable areas of the website like the email text field for the free trial.

Hick's Law, Easy Choice

Dynamite Jobs

Users of this website are usually people looking for a job unwilling to read too much about something that is not their main goal and this Dynamite Jobs brings immediately under the user eyes what they are looking for, a search bar and a bit lower a job board.

Visual Hierarchy

Four Seasons

Four Seasons web designers know what is important to their customers, knowing the prices and availability of their rooms and booking them. Therefore the check rates button is well visible and predominant in all of the viewports.
